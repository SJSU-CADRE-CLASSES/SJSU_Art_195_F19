• Diego Quiros

    Truth of Blood Woods

    Video Game
    Godot Engine

    Artist Statement

    Truth of Blood Woods is a 2D platformer made throught the Godot game engine that aims to make the public aware how women are treated in gaming culture. As an avid video game enthusiasts I see woman players treated as though they are not people. Threats, toxic, language, and being taken advantage of, and other forms of dicomfort are what keeps gamig culture male dominant. Truth of Blood Woods puts you in the shoes of Sam, and explorer who discover a video game carnival that threatens the player for being a female.

    Personal Artist Statement

    As someone who grew up playing video games most of the time, I created fond memories of these inspiring works of art making it obvious to choose art as my focus. As someone who finds joy in creating, I find joy in developing projects that bring up important concepts. I want to be an artsits that can challenge the public to stop and think.
