• Outset

    Medium: 3d printing, wood, acrylic, electronics


    Outset is a 3d zoetrope reflecting the journey of finding self-identity. While I was growing up, I was influenced by both the expectations of my family and mainstream society. I felt lost and constrained. Finding self-identity was an important lesson for me. The zoetrope depicts the process of birds growing up from their families and escaping from the cage to start their new journeys of life. This process represents people finding themselves, breaking constraints, while finally finding and pursuing their goals, following their hearts. Only we can define who we are, and this work represents that finding to me.
