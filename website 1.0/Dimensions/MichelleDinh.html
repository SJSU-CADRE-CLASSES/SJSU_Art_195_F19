• UnFiltered


    Michelle Dinh


    Artist Statement

    Language has evolved rapidly over the past few years. Every generation has acquired their own way of speech that is constantly being updated into an unofficial dictionary. Millennial speech refers to the creation of new terminologies used by newer generations (those who were born 1981-1996) and post-millenials (1997-present).

    As I interact with my own group of friends, I find myself picking up and learning new terms. These terms can be found on Urban Dictionary. I find that this unofficial dictionary continues to grow more as social media communities gain momentum in trends and coverage. The increased use of mobile devices and social media led to the development of new colloquialisms, acronyms, and emojis.

    UnFiltered features a Twitter bot attempting to express "millennial" speech. It tweets random terms from Urban Dictionary's API and takes user posts from inputted hashtag in order to fit into today's generation. The website also includes a random "on-trend" word generator and a tweet function that allows visitors to share how they would use the word. The goal of the project is to close the generational gap and spread awareness of new forms of vocal expression.
